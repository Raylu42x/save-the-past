About Us

We are the Robo Cats, a homeschooled FIRST LEGO League team based in Duvall, Washington. Most of us meet at our co-op, Labyrinth. Our team includes 10 members between the ages of 9 and 14. Together, we combine creativity, engineering, research, and teamwork to build solutions that help preserve the past one disk at a time.
